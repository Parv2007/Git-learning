Do you wonder what can you do from your side to conserve energy? Wanna know? Scroll down!
















There are three simple rules to do it. Firstly, one should remember switching off the lights and fans of a room or an appliance which will be in no use of anybody for a while. And secondly (if possible), one should try and find out better ways of conserving electricity at one's home, for example, using our techincal skills to work out a system in which the geyser gets switched off automatically when the alarm goes off. Ultimately, one should participate in as many camapaigns as one can, that support conserving energy, and one must smear the awareness about it on the face of the globe.

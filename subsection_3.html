How can we save energy?

There are three simple rules to do it:

  • Firstly, one should remember switching off the lights and fans of a room or an appliance which will be in no use of anybody for a while.
  • Secondly (if possible), one should try and find out better ways of conserving electricity at one's home, for example, using our techincal skills to work out a system in which the geyser gets switched off automatically when the alarm goes off.
  • Ultimately, one should participate in as many camapaigns as one can, that support conserving energy, and one must smear the awareness about it on the face of the globe.

Few ways to save energy:

  • Adjust your day-to-day behaviors
  • Replace your light bulbs
  • Use smart power strips
  • Install a programmable or smart thermostat
  • Purchase energy efficient appliances
  • Reduce your water heating expenses
  • Install energy efficient windows
  • Upgrade your HVAC system
  • Weatherize your home
  • Insulate your home
